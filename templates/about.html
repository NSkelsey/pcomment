Some person or organization wants to make a public statement about some question that comes in email format they get alot. All a user has to do is forward the email to the public email account at the site (general@pcomment.mailgun.org) and it will be formatted, posted and editable much like a wikipedia article (a post will only be editable by its owner). There are many things that could be put around this but I think of this as the simpliest possible way to address some citizen concerns.

The site is live so feel free to click around and send it an email if you want. You do have to register first though. I don't have user accounts yet so just use your imagination when it comes to permissions...
